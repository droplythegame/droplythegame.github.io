Do you find yourself in stressful situations, at work, studying, or just simply waiting for your spouse, kids, friends, or date?

Droply is a game designed with one goal in mind: freedom. Free of stress, free of thoughts, free of distractions, you can enjoy it anywhere, anytime, from the comfort of your small companion: your phone.

Hi there! Our names are Jason and Jad. We are the originators of Droply The Game. We created Droply in hopes that it brings you the same peace of mind that it did for us. A low-intensity, non-mission-based game that would not require any focus or commitment was the answer; a game so beautifully designed, birthed from the essence of minimalism that will help your thoughts fade away into sheer reflex.

You can freely enjoy Droply, with no game based in-app purchases other than the in-game currency. 1. What is Droply and how does it work?

Droply is a web-based 2D game aimed at helping you relax and clear your mind whenever you feel like you want to rest a bit. The concept is fairly simple: you have a water droplet character (in that case Droply at first) that is falling from the sky endlessly. All you have to do is dodge the obstacles by swiping on your screen left and right. In the process, you can protect yourself with a timed shield, as well as gather in-game currency (coins) that will offer you the possibility of unlocking new characters and landscapes (backgrounds).

2. Where can I find Droply and play it?

You can play it simply by opening up the link: https://droplythegame.com/, and bookmarking it on your phone as an independent web-page on your homescreen for quick access.

3. What are hearts?

Hearts are a way of letting you know how many tries you have left, giving you the possibility of continuing the game without restarting everything and potentially losing your progress. Whenever you have less than 3 hearts, you can very easily buy some more in our in-game shop. That way you never run out of progress!
